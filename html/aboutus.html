About Us

Jopewi Investments Limited is dedicated to provide high-quality and reliable transport services for goods on transit within and outside Kenya. Our mission is to ensure that the goods are delivered to their destinations in good condition and on time.

We carry goods with weight upto 12 tonnes per trip.

We deliver goods within 12 hours in kenya and 18 hours within countries in East Africa.


Our Vision

To be the most reliable provider of goods on transit services within east Africa region.
